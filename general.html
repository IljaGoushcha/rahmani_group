Our Story

2003 was the year when we established Rahmani Group. At that time we had a vision to change the way business is done in Afghanistan. Now almost a decade later, we are on the path to pursue our dream and have brought the change and revolution that we wanted to bring in the Corporate Industry in Afghanistan.
